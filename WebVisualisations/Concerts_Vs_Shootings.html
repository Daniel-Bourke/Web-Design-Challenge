Fillmore Concerts Vs Shootings

We investigated the possibility that certain artists concerts may have resulted in an increase in the number of shootings. The data for the Fillmore events was extracted from their website.


It appears as though the concerts from "Cypress Hill" and "Murda Beatz" had no tangible impact on the number of shootings in the following months after their respective concerts at the Fillmore.
