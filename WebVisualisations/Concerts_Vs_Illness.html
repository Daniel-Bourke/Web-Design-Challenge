Fillmore Concerts Vs Illness

We investigated the possibility that the number of events held at Phillidelphia's famous Filmore Concert Venue in a month may impact on the number of 911 "Illness" calls. "Illness" calls where defined as calls of type: "subject in pain", "Nausea/Vomiting", "Head Injury".


The data for the Fillmore events was extracted from their website


As can be seen there appears to be a positive correlation between the number of concerts and number of "illness" calls. However, a low correlation coefficient of 0.27 indicates that there is no significant linear pattern.
